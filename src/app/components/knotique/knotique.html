Meet Knotique

Beyond code, I run Knotique — a small crochet business where creativity meets craft. I design and handmake beautiful crochet items including bags, hats, home décor, and custom pieces.

What started as a hobby during the pandemic grew into a community of crochet lovers. Knotique is my way of proving that a developer can have artistic superpowers too!
